We believe in freedom that doesn’t cost the earth.

Vélory was born from a simple obsession: create the most beautiful, sustainable, and joyful electric rides — without compromise.

⚡ Our mission: Electrify every journey, responsibly.

By 2030, we aim to offset 100% of our production carbon footprint and make micro-mobility accessible to all. Every Vélory bike plants 5 trees through reforestation partners.

From a Stockholm garage to global movement

In 2020, our founders Elin & Mateo — two engineers obsessed with cycling and climate action — realized that most e-bikes were either boring or built unsustainably. They quit their jobs and started tinkering in a tiny workshop in Södermalm. The first Vélory prototype was welded from recycled aluminum and powered by a hand‑wound motor. Word spread fast.

Today, Vélory has grown into a collective of designers, riders, and activists. We partner with European suppliers, use vegan/eco materials, and every bike is assembled in our low‑impact facility in Västerås. We're not just building e-bikes — we're building a community that values clean air, quiet streets, and timeless design.
